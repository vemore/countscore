# Store Assets Directory

Place your created visual assets in this directory.

## Required Files

### App Icon
- **File**: `icon_512.png`
- **Size**: 512 × 512 pixels
- **Format**: 32-bit PNG with alpha
- **Max Size**: 1 MB

### Feature Graphic
- **File**: `feature_graphic.png` (or `.jpg`)
- **Size**: 1024 × 500 pixels
- **Format**: JPEG or 24-bit PNG (no alpha)

### Screenshots

#### Phone Screenshots (Required)
Place in `screenshots/phone/`:
- **Quantity**: Minimum 2, recommended 4-8
- **Resolution**: 1080 × 1920 pixels recommended
- **Format**: JPEG or PNG
- **Naming**: `01_main_screen.png`, `02_players.png`, etc.

#### Tablet Screenshots (Optional)
Place in `screenshots/tablet/`:
- **Quantity**: 1-8 screenshots
- **Resolution**: 1536 × 2048 pixels recommended
- **Format**: JPEG or PNG
